This is an online learning and testing platform. It allows teachers or administrators to set up virtual classrooms, create timed multiple-choice tests, and track student performance. Students can join these classes, take tests, and instantly see their grades.

Here is how it works for everyone involved:

For Administrators (Teachers & Staff)
The platform gives you a secure, private area to manage your students and tests.

Overview Dashboard: As soon as you log in, you will see a quick summary of what is happening—how many students are registered, how many exams are active, and who is waiting to be approved.

Manage Classrooms & Students: You can create distinct classrooms for different subjects or groups. When a student signs up, you get to approve or reject them before they can see any course materials.

Create Exams: You can easily build multiple-choice tests, group them into categories, and set strict time limits for each test. You can hide or show these exams to students with the click of a button.

Track Performance: You have full access to student records. You can see when they took a test, what their final score was, and exactly which questions they got right or wrong.

For Students
Students get a simple, easy-to-use portal to take their tests and track their own progress.

Sign Up & Enroll: Students can create their own accounts and request to join specific classrooms.

Take Timed Tests: Once you approve them, they can see the exams available for their class. When they start a test, a live timer counts down. If time runs out, the test submits itself automatically so they never lose their work.

Instant Results: The moment they finish an exam, the app grades it and shows them their score immediately.

Review Past Work: Students can look back at their history to see how they performed on older tests.

Profile Settings: They can update their personal information, like their email or phone number.

General Platform Features
These are the built-in features that ensure the app runs smoothly for everyone:

Works on Any Device: The app automatically adjusts to look great and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Search Engine Friendly: The pages are designed so that people can easily find the platform using search engines like Google.

High Security: The platform uses modern, standard security measures to protect passwords, keep user data private, and prevent hackers from interfering with the system.

Standard Website Pages: It includes standard informational pages, like an "About Us" section and a Contact page so users can easily get in touch.
